2019 Fall Champions

Flight championship trophies can be picked up starting Dec 11th from Third Base Sports and Trophies, Monday-Friday 9-5, or Saturdays, 12/14 and 12/21 from 9-12pm. Third Base is shut down from vacation from Dec. 24th to Jan. 5th, 2020.  The list of winners is attached and can be found on the SJSL.org web site, under the Admin tab.

Third Base Sports (856) 665-0114, info@thirdbasesports.com, 1960 Old Cuthbert Road, Suite 120, Cherry Hill, NJ 08034.

Take Chapel Ave from Kings Highway to the end turn left at Old Cuthbert Road.  1960 is down on left (third office).  From Route 70 west turn right on Old Cuthbert Road.  It is just before the 295 North exit. 

The PAST Champions for all flights from U11 through U19 are celebrated here.

For older results, the U11 through U14 Champions will be listed by Class (age group according to NJYS) and broken down by flight and age level going from U14 back to U11.  For example, the current U11 class is known as Class 2003.  They include players from Aug. 1, 2003 to July 31, 2004. The Class of 1999 includes players with birth dates between Aug. 1, 1999 and July 31, 2000.  They played U14 in the Spring of 2014 and back to U11 in the Fall of 2010.  All past champions are listed.

As older records are converted, champions from further years back will be included.

Congratulations to ALL teams!
