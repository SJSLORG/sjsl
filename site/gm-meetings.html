SJSL General Membership Meetings are generally held on the 3rd Thursday of the month at 8:00PM, unless otherwise specified. The location of meetings is subject to change and members are encouraged to check their email and the league website for any changes. Generally, meetings will be held at the Hotel ML located at 915 Rte. 73, Mount Laurel, NJ 08054.

From 295: Take Exit 36A (Route 73 South). The Hotel ML will be on your left. Take Jug Handle for U-turn at first traffic light.

From New Jersey Turnpike: Take Exit 4. After the toll, follow signs for Philadelphia/Camden and Route 73 North. The Hotel ML will be on your right about one quarter of a mile down.

From Atlantic City Expressway: Take Exit 31 (Route 73 North) for approximately 21 miles. The Hotel ML will be on your right.
