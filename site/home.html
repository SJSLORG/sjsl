South Jersey Strong

April 23rd, 2020

To all 2020 SJSL Spring Season Teams,

Please know that our thoughts are with each and every one of you in these difficult and challenging times. We know that your health and safety are your first priorities, and that your teams and players are always on your minds. Following the direction of the US Soccer Federation and US Club Soccer, as well as the protections imposed by Governor Murphy, it is no longer practical to expect youth games to be played in April or May. Accordingly, all spring league games for 2020 have been cancelled. We recognize that there is still continued uncertainty regarding any return to future activity. As we obtain more information, we will pass it on to you. We will send out future emails regarding spring registration credits and fall registration information once we understand what restrictions may remain in place.

We will get through this and we will be stronger by doing it together. As a thank you to our clubs and teams, to show unity and support for everyone, and to keep soccer in everyone’s thoughts, the South Jersey Soccer League is offering a FREE “South Jersey Strong” (see logo at the bottom) white t-shirt printed for all rostered players and one coach for each team that was registered to play in the SJSL 2020 Spring season. There will be NO COST to anyone.

Considering that we have approximately 7,000 players registered for the spring season, the following procedure will be used to collect the necessary data to accomplish this task.

The SJSL Executive Board thanks everyone for their past and continued support. Once the all clear has been issued by US Soccer and the state of New Jersey, SJSL will communicate to everyone our plans for the fall season. Stay safe, stay strong.

Thank you!

SJSL Executive Board

SJSL Coronavirus Update

March 12th, 2020

To everyone - having reviewed the recommendations from the US Soccer Federation and US Club Soccer, and in light of the concerns raised by our member clubs, the South Jersey Soccer League (Boys) has decided the following:

  1. All games for the month of March (3/15, 3/22, 3/29) are suspended, including any scheduled make up games.
  2. We will reevaluate our position and email all member clubs on Wednesday, March 26th regarding whether the games scheduled for 3/29 will remain suspended or will be played.
  3. Round 2 games (3/15) will not be made up.
  4. Round 3 games (3/22) will not be made up if the league also cancels Round 4 games (3/29), otherwise a decision on the round makeup will be made at a later date.
  5. Matches played in violation of this policy will not be sanctioned by the league.

We encourage all of our members to stay educated on the precautions and proactive measures published by the CDC, WHO and NJ Department of Health. Player and family safety should be our first priority. Thank you for your support and understanding in this challenging time.

SJSL Executive Board
