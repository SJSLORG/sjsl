Lace Up 4 Pediatric Cancer

South Jersey Soccer League is teaming up with Go4theGoal to raise funds and awareness for kids with cancer here in New Jersey!

As part of Go4theGoal’s Lace Up 4 Pediatric Cancer® initiative, we are encouraging all teams to purchase gold laces to wear during one of your games.

  • Standard laces are available in a variety of sizes for $5/pair with $4 from every pair going back to the cause.
  • Thinner 54” inch laces are also available for cleats with smaller eyelets. Thinner laces are $7/pair with $5 from every pair going back to the cause.
  • Laces can be purchased by contacting NickKelleher@Go4theGoal.org or at Go4theGoal.org.
  • Shipping has an $8 flat rate so make sure to order as a team!
  • Post pictures of your team in their laces and tag @Go4theGoal!

2021 Inaugural Jim Miller Cup

SJSL is proud to introduce the Inaugural Jim Miller Cup!

Jim Miller has been a fixture on the SJSL Board since the 1970s and left after the Spring 2020 season. Jim was so dedicated to serving the members, he continued being on the board after he moved from South Jersey to Lancaster County, PA. This cup is to honor his dedication to the clubs, coaches, and players in South Jersey.
